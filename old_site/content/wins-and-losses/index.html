Wins and Losses

Post Mortem: dataFundamentals 2014, Pete and Jeff

After failing to get airborne in a go at the consumer software market, and inadvertantly killing our corporate services offerings through a move from corporate centric Dallas to startup centric Austin - an otherwise delightful move - dataFundamentals is formally shuttering it's contracting offerings in favor of seeking revenues through long term W2 employment in the Austin market.

In many respects - this is the ultimate loss. But it has been a joyous and event filled ride. Thanks to our many supporters for allowing us to participate in their projects along the way.

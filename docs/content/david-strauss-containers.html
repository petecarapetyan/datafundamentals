David Strauss on Containers

Submitted by Jeff Carapetyan on Fri, 06/06/2014 - 12:14

High Turnout at Austin DevOps Group

This week, we had  David Strauss come in and speak about the use of Linux containers at his company.

David Strauss is the founder of Pantheon Systems and we had 166 people RSVP to this meetup when we announced who it was.

Are We Ready for Containers Yet?

While I love the concept of linux containers, I have had doubts on their readiness for commercial application. One fact completely changed that perception of things.

David has implemented 1 million containers in production. 1 Million! That is proof to me that, not only am I wrong, but a lot of this is ready for production.
